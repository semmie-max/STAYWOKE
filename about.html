Who We Are

Fashion as an
Expression

At StayWoke, we believe that fashion is an expression of individuality and artistry. We strive to create exquisite garments that embody sophistication and elevate the wearer's unique style.

Our dedication to craftsmanship and attention to detail ensure that every piece in our collection is a masterpiece — designed not just to be worn, but to be felt.

The Founder

Anjola
Hezekiah

In 2024, StayWoke was founded by Anjola Hezekiah — a visionary designer with a passion for luxury fashion. Inspired by the flair for street fashion, Anjola Hezekiah set out to create a brand that would redefine elegance and set new standards of unmatched quality.

Each masterpiece was designed to proselytize street fashion with love and true devotedness — across borders and beyond.
